Milk Soda

Milk Soda Club is a hypothetical student organization dedicated to drinking a blend of whole milk and whatever sugary soda is on hand, conceptualized by a friend of mine. Taking the club to be an opportunity for brand identity, I have created a potential visual identity for a woefully nonexistent association of milk soda drinkers.
